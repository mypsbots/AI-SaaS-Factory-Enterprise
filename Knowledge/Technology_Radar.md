# KNOWLEDGE — Technology Radar

> Guidance on adopting, trialling, or retiring technologies.

---

## 1. Summary

Guidance on adopting, trialling, or retiring technologies.

---

## 2. Context

Curated reference knowledge for teams building SaaS products with ASFE.

---

## 3. Key Concepts

- Adopt / Trial / Assess / Hold rings
- Techniques, Tools, Platforms, Languages & Frameworks
- Fitness functions
- Total cost of ownership

---

## 4. Guidance

- Evaluate against principles and standards before adoption.
- Prefer boring, well-supported technology for core systems.
- Document decisions as ADRs.

---

## 5. Pitfalls

- Resume-driven technology choices.
- Adopting unproven tech for critical paths.
- No exit strategy for retired tech.
