# PLAYBOOK — Feature Launch Playbook

> Repeatable operational procedure owned by the Product Manager.

---

## 1. Purpose

Provide a consistent, tested procedure for **Feature Launch Playbook**.

---

## 2. Objective

Launch features successfully with measurable outcomes.

---

## 3. Trigger

A feature reaches launch readiness.

---

## 4. Roles

- Product Manager
- Product Marketing
- Engineering
- Customer Success

---

## 5. Preconditions

- Required access, tooling, and runbooks are available.
- Stakeholders and communication channels are known.

---

## 6. Procedure

1. Confirm success metrics and instrumentation.
2. Validate readiness across eng, docs, support, and GTM.
3. Roll out progressively behind flags.
4. Monitor adoption and quality signals.
5. Enable sales/support and publish materials.
6. Review outcomes against targets.
7. Iterate or roll back based on data.

---

## 7. Decision Points

- Escalate when impact or scope exceeds the responder's authority.
- Choose mitigate-forward vs. roll back based on predefined criteria.

---

## 8. Communication Plan

- Notify stakeholders at start, on status changes, and at resolution.
- Use the designated incident/comms channel; keep a timeline.

---

## 9. Rollback / Recovery

- Maintain a tested rollback or recovery path.
- Verify recovery against SLIs and smoke tests.

---

## 10. Post-Procedure Review

- Run a blameless review.
- Log owned, time-bound action items.
- Update this playbook with lessons learned.

---

## 11. Validation Checklist

- [ ] Trigger and roles are clear
- [ ] Each step is actionable and tested
- [ ] Communication plan executed
- [ ] Rollback/recovery verified
- [ ] Post-review completed with actions
- [ ] Metadata complete

---

## 12. Metrics

- Time to execute
- Success rate
- Recurrence rate
- Action-item closure
